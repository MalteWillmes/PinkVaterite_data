# PinkVaterite_data

This repository provides the data and scripts for peer review of "Widespread  high  prevalence  of  vaterite  in  pink  salmon  (Oncorhynchus  gorbuscha) otoliths".

The  samples  from  the  North  Atlantic  were  collected  across  a  broad  geographic  range,  including  Norway,  Greenland,  Iceland,  the  Faroe  Islands,  Great  Britain  and  Ireland. Collections were carried out by a variety of partners and parts of the dataset are part of the PinkSIES project. The  samples  from  the  Pacific  region  were  provided  by  the  Alaska  Department  of  Fish  and  Game. For re-use of data please contact the lead author Frode Pedersen.
